# Operational Check Default Images

This folder contains default images for the 8 operational quality check questions.

## Required Images (1200x1200px recommended)

1. **exterior.jpg** - Restaurant exterior and patio area
2. **interior.jpg** - Interior dining area
3. **welcome.jpg** - Entrance/host stand area
4. **staff-service.jpg** - Staff serving customers
5. **restroom.jpg** - Clean restroom area
6. **food-safety.jpg** - Kitchen/food preparation area
7. **ambience.jpg** - Dining atmosphere/decor
8. **staff-care.jpg** - Staff interacting with customers

## Image Guidelines

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Size**: 1200x1200px (or similar square aspect ratio)
- **Quality**: High resolution, well-lit, professional
- **Style**: Warm, inviting, restaurant-appropriate
- **Rights**: Ensure you have proper licensing/rights to use these images

## Sources for Free Stock Photos

- [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/s/photos/restaurant) - Free high-quality photos
- [Pexels](https://www.pexels.com/search/restaurant/) - Free stock photos
- [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/images/search/restaurant/) - Free images

## Temporary Placeholder

If images are missing, the app will fall back to showing the fortune cookie image until proper images are added.
